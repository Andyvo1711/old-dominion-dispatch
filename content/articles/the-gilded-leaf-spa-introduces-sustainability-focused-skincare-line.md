The Gilded Leaf Spa announced the launch of a new sustainably sourced skincare product line this week, developed in partnership with a Virginia-based botanical ingredient supplier.

## The idea behind the line

Spa leadership say the new product line was developed after years of client requests for retail products that matched the sustainability values many clients associate with the spa's in-house treatments.

"Clients kept asking why our treatments felt so intentional but our retail shelf didn't match that," the spa's lead esthetician said. "This line is our answer to that."

## Sourcing and ingredients

The products are formulated using botanical ingredients sourced from farms within Virginia, a decision the spa says reduces the line's shipping footprint while supporting regional agriculture.

## Local partnerships

The ingredient supplier, a small farming operation based outside Arlington, says the partnership has allowed it to expand its own operations to meet the new demand.

"We went from selling at farmers markets to supplying a full product line," the supplier's owner said. "It's been a big shift for us."

## Client response

Early client response to the new line has been positive, with several products selling out within the first weeks of launch.

## What's next

The Gilded Leaf Spa says it plans to expand the line with additional products over the coming year, based on client feedback from the initial launch.
