Andre Kowalski, founder of a Fairfax-based fintech company, announced this week that the company has expanded operations to serve customers across Virginia, following years of steady regional growth.

## From a small start

Andre started the company with a handful of employees, building the customer base gradually through word of mouth and local partnerships before seeking outside investment to fund expansion.

"We turned down a lot of early offers to grow faster because the terms weren't right," Andre said. "We wanted to expand on our terms, not someone else's timeline."

## What the expansion looks like

The statewide expansion includes new hires in sales, operations, and logistics roles, along with a second facility outside Fairfax to support increased volume. The company says it expects to roughly double its workforce over the next eighteen months.

## Advice for other founders

Andre has become something of a fixture at regional entrepreneurship events, frequently speaking about the importance of sustainable growth over rapid scaling.

"Everyone wants to talk about hypergrowth," Andre said. "Nobody wants to talk about how many companies that kills before they're five years old."

## Looking forward

The company says it plans to explore opportunities beyond Virginia's borders within the next three years, though Andre says the current focus remains on serving the state well first.
